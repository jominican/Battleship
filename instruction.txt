How to play Battleship:

1) Click on player's side of the board to place ships. There are 4 types of ships: 1 Aircraft Carrier, 1 Battleship, 1 Cruiser, and 2 Destroyers
2) Click on a ship already placed will remove the ship
3) Once all ships have been placed, click on the Select File button to select a .battle file for the computer
4) Click "Start" to begin game
5) Click on the computer's side of the board to hit a ship
6) Game will end once a player/computer's ships have all been hit.
